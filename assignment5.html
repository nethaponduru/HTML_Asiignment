1. Bahubali: The Beginning
    • Released in 2015
    • Main Actors
      • Prabhas
      • Anushka
      • Rana
    • The film follows Sivudu, an adventurous young man who helps his love Avantika rescue Devasena, the former queen of Mahishmati who is now a prisoner under the tyrannical rule of king Bhallaladeva

  2. Bahubali: The Conclusion
    • Released in 2017
    • Main Actors
      • Prabhas
      • Anushka
      • Rana
    • This film is the follow-up to Baahubali: The Beginning, serving as both a sequel and a prequel.The film is set in medieval India and follows the sibling rivalry between Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva; the latter conspires against the former and has him killed by Kattappa. Years later, Amarendra's son returns to avenge his death.

  3. KGF Chapter 1
    • Released in 2018
    • Main Actors
      • Yash
      • Srinidhi Shetty
      • Ramachandra Raju
    • In the 1970s, a gangster named Rocky goes undercover as a slave to assassinate the owner of a notorious gold mine known as the Kolar Gold Fields.

  4. KGF Chapter 2
    • Released in 2022
    • Main Actors
      • Yash
      • Srinidhi Shetty
      • Ramachandra Raju
    • In the blood-soaked Kolar Gold Fields, Rocky's name strikes fear into his foes, while the government sees him as a threat to law and order. Rocky must battle threats from all sides for unchallenged supremacy.

  5. Pushpa: The Raise
    • Released in 2021
    • Main Actors
      • Allu Arjun
      • Rashmika Mandhana
      • Fahadh Fasil
    • A labourer rises through the ranks of a red sandal smuggling syndicate, making some powerful enemies in the process.
